Fuel Moisture Physics

1. The "Boiling Point" Delay: Fire cannot consume fuel until internal water evaporates. High moisture creates a "drying" buffer (yellow/brown trees) before ignition.

2. Ignition Threshold: Drying starts at 5x base moisture; ignition occurs at 0. A 30% forest is roughly 3x harder to ignite than a 10% forest.
