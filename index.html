Fuel Moisture Physics

In this simulator, fuel moisture represents the energy barrier to ignition.

  • The Boiling Point: Heat must evaporate moisture before combustion begins. Higher moisture creates a longer "drying" state (yellow-brown trees).
  • Spread Thresholds: Above 30% moisture, the fire typically enters the "Extinction Point" where spread cannot be sustained.
  • Drought Influence: At 5-10%, ignition is nearly instantaneous.
